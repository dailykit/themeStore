Frequently Asked Questions

What are Rio Fit Meals?

Rio Fit Meals are flavorful, healthy, balanced fully cooked meals delivered to you, and ready to eat in minutes. The special chef-inspired sous-vide preparation vacuum-seals the flavor and locks in the freshness. Rio Fit Meals can be easily reheated by placing each pouch in boiling water.

Do I have to cook the Rio Fit Meals?

No. Rio Fit Meals are fully cooked and already prepared. You only need to reheat the dish by placing the vacuum-sealed food pouches in boiling water for the designated number of minutes, noted on the package.

How are Rio Fit Meals prepared?

The freshly made, balanced meals are prepared by award-winning chef, Rodrigo Souza. Delicious Rio Fit Meals are made (to-order) with love, then delivered the same day (every Monday). We use local produce when possible, from responsible farms and sources.

How does the Rio Fit Meals plan work?

Sign up to receive 5 or 10 (or more) meals per week. Select your dishes online from the weekly offerings. Pay monthly for the designated meal quantity.

Who is Rodrigo Souza?

Rodrigo is an award-winning chef (Best of Worcester) and owner of Comeketo Brazilian Steakhouse & Catering in Leominster, MA. He loves to feed his family and friends with exceptionally delicious meals. Rodrigo’s passion for healthy cooking and helping others, fueled this new concept that brings restaurant-quality dishes at home, saving you time and money.

How do Rio Fit Meals save me time and money?

We use fresh ingredients from responsible sources. By saving you time to prepare a well-balanced meal with sides, you spend more quality time with the family. The chef-prepared meals are comparable.

How do I get my Rio Fit Meals?

Rio Fit Meals are delivered to your home or your business, on a weekly basis. All meals are made on Mondays and delivered to you on the same day. Delivery fees vary based on location (from Leominster, MA).

What’s the story behind Rio Fit Meals?

Rio Fit Meals’ owner and executive chef, Rodrigo Souza, tried many of the existing sous-vide prepared meals currently in the market. He found the offerings to be bland, unappetizing food and a huge disappointment. Rodrigo immediately knew his award-winning food could beat the competition with better tasting, fresh and healthy meals. At that moment, the Rio Fit Meals brand was born.

Rio Fit Meals are flavorful, healthy, well-balanced meals delivered to your door and ready to eat in minutes. The special chef-inspired sous-vide method vacuum seals the flavor to keep it tasting the way the chef intended.
